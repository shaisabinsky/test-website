About Lil Tjay

Tione Jayden Merritt, better known as Lil Tjay, is an American rapper, singer, and songwriter from the Bronx, New York. His collaboration with Polo G, Pop Out peaked at 11 on the Billboard Hot 100 and his song "Leaked" received 44.4 million plays on SoundCloud, which led to his signing with Columbia Records.
