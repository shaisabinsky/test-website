Characters from Star Wars The Rise of Skywalker.

Rey

Rey is the main protaganist and very powerful when she uses the force. Once a scavenger on the planet Jakku she now helps the resistance fight the First Order so they can defeat them once and for all.

Kylo Ren

Kylo Ren is a strong dark warrior who leads the First Order. He is trying to destory the New Republic, the Resistance, and the Legacy of the Jedi.

Finn

Finn was trained since birth to serve the First Order as a stormtrooper but after his consciene was awakened he fled from the First Order and joined the Resistance to help stop th First Order.
